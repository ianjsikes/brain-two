# Giffyglyph Monster Maker

## 1. Pick a Level

Ditch CR. Monster levels match one-to-one with player levels. Look up basic stats on table.

## 2. Apply a Role

- Controller
  Disorient, move, debuff
- Defender
  Shield, block, tank
- Lurker
  Hide, glass cannon
- Scout
  High speed and perception
- Sniper
  Long range and safe
- Striker
  Dish out and take damage
- Supporter
  Buffs and heals. Squishy

Each role modifies stats.
